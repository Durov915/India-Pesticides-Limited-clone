What we do

India Pesticides Limited is one of the fastest growing globally operating chemical manufactures in India.Set up in 1984, India Pesticides has pioneered the manufacturing of both Technicals & Formulations in Agro-Chemicals and Active Pharma Ingredients for Pharma industry.

We are the sole Indian manufacturer and among top five manufacturers globally for several Technicals both in Fungicides and Herbicides.Our ranges of diversified portfolio of niche and quality products along with value-added services make us to be a strategic supplier for US, Australia, Europe, Africa, South America and Asia.
